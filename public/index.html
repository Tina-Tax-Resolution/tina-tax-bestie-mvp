Import Notes

Recommended record types: cash_income, cash_expense, noncash_income, crypto_income, crypto_expense.

For brand gifts, barter, products, trips, or items received in exchange for services, posts, reviews, promotion, or content, enter the fair market value in amount_usd and explain the FMV method.

For crypto, enter the USD value at receipt or payment time, plus token amount and transaction hash if available.

For multiple businesses, include business_id or choose the business before manual entry. Use allocation_percent and shared_use_note for shared items.
