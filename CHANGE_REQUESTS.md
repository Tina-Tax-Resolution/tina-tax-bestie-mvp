# Tax Bestie Change Requests

## Current Priority

1. Keep each business/activity separate.
   - Records must filter by exact business ID.
   - Selecting a new business/activity must clear the prior business view immediately.
   - Prior business income, expenses, alerts, and records should not appear under a new activity.

2. Save & Continue Later after each record.
   - Add a clear post-save option for contemporaneous records.
   - Avoid wording that implies the tax year is finished.
   - Preferred wording: "Save & Continue Later" and "Review This Year So Far."

3. Evidence status on the Progress page.
   - Keep the proof skip option.
   - Show whether proof was attached, skipped for now, note-only, or FMV support missing.
   - Suggested labels: "Proof attached", "No receipt/proof added yet", "Note saved, no file attached", "FMV support not attached yet."

4. Retention and soft delete.
   - Delete should remove a record from active totals but keep it in deleted/archived records.
   - Attached receipts/proof should stay with the deleted record unless intentionally permanently deleted.
   - Retention guidance should be based on IRC section 6001, IRC section 6501, and IRS recordkeeping guidance.

5. Electronic and digital receipts.
   - Treat screenshots, PDFs, emails saved as PDF, platform reports, bank/card confirmations, invoices, contracts, and FMV screenshots as support documents.
   - Save upload status, file name, upload date/time, tax year, business/activity, and whether proof was attached at entry or added later.

6. Prior-year totals without tax return upload.
   - Do not ask users to upload tax returns.
   - Do not make 1099 upload a named feature.
   - Allow optional prior-year backfill using Schedule C totals: income, expenses, net profit/loss, and optional SE tax note.
   - Allow optional support uploads such as bank statements, platform reports, receipts, or bookkeeping exports.

7. Record Details wording.
   - Replace "Record Strength" because it can mislead users into thinking the app judged proof quality.
   - Show basic detail completeness separately from receipt/proof attachment status.
   - If no receipts or proof files were attached, say that clearly even when records have dates, amounts, notes, or completed 9-question answers.

8. No client relationship disclaimer.
   - Add clear wording that using Tax Bestie does not create a client relationship with Tina Your Tax Bestie LLC or Tina's Tax Resolution LLC.
   - Keep this tied to the educational/Circular 230 disclaimer so users understand the app is a record-organization tool, not representation or individualized tax advice.

## Back Burner / Later

1. Batch import from other platforms.
   - CSV imports, bank/platform exports, receipt summaries, or bookkeeping-app exports.
   - Imported data should be labeled "Imported" or "Prior-year summary" so it is not confused with contemporaneous entries.

2. Retention labels.
   - Show "Retain until at least..." by record type.
   - Asset/equipment records should flag longer retention through disposal year plus limitation period.

3. Evidence dashboard.
   - Show proof gaps by tax year and business/activity.
   - Highlight missing proof more strongly when losses repeat or expenses are high.

4. User tester package.
   - Add a simple tester instruction page before sharing the Render link.
   - Include MVP disclaimer and reset instructions.
